Item #: ████

Object Class: Pending Euclid Keter

Special Containment Procedures:

SCP-████ is to be kept within a standard 3x3x3m storage container 3x3x3m airtight steel chamber constantly pressurized to 3,500 hPa with chlorine gas within Armed Bio-Containment Area-14. All personnel interacting with SCP-███ must don standard biohazard protection gear No interaction with SCP-████ is permitted unless absolutely necessary. SCP-███ may not be removed from its chamber unless it is being removed for testing, with prior clearance from Level 5 personnel. All documentation of SCP-████ should be held on secure external SCiPNet servers to prevent proliferation of memetic hazards.

Description:

SCP-████ is a 2006 Honda Civic. Notably, SCP-████ is devoid of all forms of identification (serial numbers, license plates, etc.) SCP-████'s only primary anamalous property is its ability to manifest high quantities of Bacillus Anthracis (Anthrax) bacteria on its surface. The bacteria generated by SCP-████ are able to regenerate at extremely high rates. Bacteria on its surface has been noted to regenerate to lethal levels within ten minutes of total sterilization of SCP-████. SCP-████ also has the ability to generate memetic hazards and either memetically transmit said hazards into the conciousness of attackers or cause the appearance of said memes on its surface as a form of defense. As of 20██-5-11, SCP-████ has been constantly using this defense mechanism for the past 2██ days.

Addendum - Incident 9/5/███

On September 5, 20██ an attempt was made by Area-14 personnel to neutralize SCP-████. SCP-████ was transported by D-Class personnel to the Area's incinerator. Upon arrival at the incinerator, SCP-████ activated its defense mechanism. ████████ ████ ████████████ killed ███ Area-14 security personnel by ████████ ██████████ neural functions. Upon recontainment, SCP-████ memetically transmitted the following message:

Biology of target subjects incompatible with mission objective. You are exempt from salvation.
