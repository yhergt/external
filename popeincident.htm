Addendum - Incident 17-03-██

On 17-03-██, SCP-343 was brought to SCP-33██ for cross-testing. Attached is the transcript of the events that followed.

  • 0930 hrs SCP-343 brought in for cross-testing
  • 0931 hrs Handling staff go silent
  • 0935 hrs All cars on Site ██, except those manufactured by Honda spontaneously combust.
  • 0942 hrs Reports reach the Foundation of cars spontaneously combusting across the world
  • 0945 hrs Various General Motors plants explode. Witnesses report seeing a "bright white light" ascending above the site of explosion
  • 0949 hrs Similar events happen at other manufacturers' plants, with the exception of Honda plants
  • 0953 hrs Mass Internet blackouts. All devices previously connected to the Internet begin playing cognitohazardous Honda advertisments causing the urge in viewers to worship Honda-manufactured vehicles and profess Catholic faith.
  • 0954 hrs Similar effects manifest in SCiPNet terminals. Majority of Foundation personnel compromised
  • 1004 hrs O5 Council convenes, and classifies current events as a CK-Class reality restructuring scenario. Missiles with amnestic warheads launched towards major urban centers by surviving Foundation personnel.
  • 1025 hrs O5 Council attempts to evacuate to Antarctica by helicopter.
  • 1045 hrs Amnestic missiles fly off target and begin impacting automotive manufacturing plants.
  • 1102 hrs All aircraft carrying O5 Council members impact various automotive manufacturing plants.
  • 1120 hrs Foundation personnel discover SCP-2000 has been converted to a Honda manufacturing facility
  • 1129 hrs SCP-343 begins ascension to an immaterial form. An XK-class event is forecasted to occur within the next 10 hours.

This document was composed and transmitted through SCP-████ on March 3, 20██ at 1300 hours GMT by order of the Administrator.
